Our Expertise

Legal services tailored
to your needs.

Whether you are buying your first home, selling a property, or remortgaging, our conveyancing team guides you through every step. We handle searches, contracts, and completion with clear communication throughout so there are no nasty surprises on moving day.
A properly drafted will is the single most important thing you can do to protect your family. We also help executors navigate the probate process, from applying for the grant through to distributing the estate, taking the burden off your shoulders during a difficult time.
Divorce, separation, children arrangements, and financial settlements — handled with sensitivity and discretion. We work to reach fair outcomes through negotiation wherever possible, keeping matters out of court and costs under control.
From contract disputes and debt recovery to boundary disagreements and professional negligence claims, our litigation team fights your corner. We pursue resolution through mediation, negotiation, or court proceedings as the situation demands.
Practical legal advice for businesses of all sizes. We help with company formations, shareholder agreements, commercial contracts, terms of business, and corporate restructuring — keeping your business on solid legal ground.
Advising both employers and employees on contracts, disciplinary procedures, redundancies, settlement agreements, and tribunal claims. We aim for pragmatic solutions that protect your interests and avoid unnecessary conflict.
Lasting Powers of Attorney for health and welfare, and for property and financial affairs. We help you put these vital documents in place while you have capacity, giving you and your family peace of mind for the future.
From establishing charitable trusts and CIOs to advising trustees on their duties and governance, we bring specialist knowledge to the charitable sector. We also advise on private trusts for wealth protection and succession planning.
Our Notary Public, regulated by the Faculty Office of the Archbishop of Canterbury, can authenticate documents for use abroad, administer oaths, and certify copies. Essential for international property transactions, powers of attorney, and overseas legal matters.
Protecting your intellectual property rights and your reputation. We advise on trade marks, copyright, confidential information, and defamation claims — whether you need to enforce your rights or defend against allegations.
